Service

Big Event is one of the largest service projects in the country, where thousands of Aggies come together and help residents out by doing yard work, painting, and maintenence. I also participated in Big Event and spent a day cleaning out the backyard of a resident by trimming trees and bushes, raking all the leaves, and washing the walls of the home.

The Houston Food Bank is a great foundation that helps feed millions in not just Houston, but a large portion of Texas. I have spent many hours volunteering at the Houston Food Bank, with each time being as rewarding as the last. I have helped with distribution, packaging, sorting, and recycling. They always need as much help as they can get, and you should volunteer if you can!

Future Contributions

I would love to also help out more with A&M events, such as local coding competitions, hackathons, and reaching out to the younger generation to get them more excited about learning in general. I plan to continue with Big Event, and hope to help out the loving community here in College Station!
